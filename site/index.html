NES Tetris

Welcome to our homepage! Ever heard of Tetris? It's an iconic puzzle game that's been captivating players for decades. In Tetris, colorful blocks fall from the top of the screen, and your task is to rotate and maneuver them to create solid rows without any gaps. It sounds simple, but as the blocks fall faster and the levels progress, the challenge ramps up! Dive into the world of Tetris and experience the addictive fun that has made it a timeless classic.
